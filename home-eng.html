Hello! I am Carlos Bleycher, screenwriter, editor and creative consultant.


I started writing comedy 12 years ago. I began writing sitcoms and then focused on animation. I have been head of content and screenwriter of Zumbastico Studios production company for the past 6 years.   

I was head writer for “Zumbastico Fantastico”, “Horatio & The Plasticines, “Morrison Hostel” and “Paper Port” wich were broadcasted by international T.V networks like Cartoon Network (LA), Gloob (Brazil), Paka-Paka (Argentina), Señal Colombia, HBO (Southeast Asia), ABC (Australia), Sony (India) and Netflix.


“Paper Port” (“Puerto Papel”/“Porto Papel”) won important awards of the audiovisual industry and children’s content like the India Catalina in Colombia, Prix Jeunesse in Germany and TAL, award for best content in public television in Latin America.  

I’m the creator of “Cagemates”, an original animated series that won the Disney Channel Prize in MIFA during the international animation festival of Annecy 2013, France. 


I have been head writer in 5 animation series and written, edited, created, thought up, destroyed, rewritten and advised more than 10 animation and comedy shows - from preschool  to adult content, web series to feature films. 

I taught “Narrative for Children and Teenagers” at UNIACC University (Chile) for 3 years and was invited to facilitate the “Animation Scriptwriting Workshop” at ICAIC in La Habana, Cuba.


I currently live in Barcelona.


You can contact me at carlos@bleycher.com
