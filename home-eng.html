Hello! I am Carlos Bleycher, screenwriter, editor and creative consultant.

I started writing comedy 12 years ago. Initially, I wrote sitcoms and then switched my concentration to animation and children’s content. For the past 6 years, I have been the Head Screenwriter responsible for content and screenwriting at Zumbastico Studios production company.   

I was the lead writer for “Zumbastico Fantastico”, “Horatio & The Plasticines, “Morrison Hostel” and “Paper Port”. These shows have been and are currently being broadcasted by international television networks including the Cartoon Network (LA), Gloob (Brazil), Paka-Paka (Argentina), Señal (Colombia), HBO (Southeast Asia), ABC (Australia), Sony (India) and Netflix. 

“Paper Port” (“Puerto Papel”/“Porto Papel”) has won important awards in the audiovisual industry and for children’s content: the India Catalina in Colombia, Prix Jeunesse in Germany and TAL award in Latin America for best content in public television.  

I am the creator of “Cagemates”, an original animated series that won the 2013 Disney Channel Prize in MIFA during the Annecy International Animation Film Festival in France. 

I have been the lead writer of 5 animation series. I have written, edited, created, thought up, destroyed, rewritten and advised more than 10 animation and comedy shows ranging in ages and content from preschool to adult, and from a web series to feature films.  

For 3 years, I taught “Narrative for Children and Teenagers” at UNIACC University (Chile). I was invited to facilitate and participated in the “Animation Scriptwriting Workshop” at ICAIC in Havana, Cuba, with Gabriel Osorio.

I’m a spanish native speaker, but I have worked developing and writing projects from english speaking countries… so don’t be shy :D

I currently live in Barcelona.
